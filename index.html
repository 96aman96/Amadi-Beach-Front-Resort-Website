The Grand Resort

We are a newly opened beach paradise, striving relentlessly for unparalled customer satisfaction in the heart of Goa

Gourmet Dining

With over 500 menu items and new specials every now and then you will never run out of things to try. Plus we regularly run omellete bars, Teppenyaki Grills and other exquisite dining events.

Swimming Pool & Beach

We are on the beach so it takes no more than 10 seconds to get out of your room and into the blissful Arabian Sea. We also have a pool when you don''t want to have sandy feet.

Fully Equipped Villas

We provide fully equipped villas inclusive of services like free Wi-Fi, air conditioning,turndown service ,hot/cold running water, 18 inch shower, in-room safety deposit lockers, laundry services etc.

Speical Events

We are always running events for our guests to keep them entertained. Notable events include movie nights, karaoke evenings, triva game shows, and a host of other fun events.

2 Michelin Stars Cooking

Chef Bharat and his team keep experimenting with flavours and spices from across the world to tantalize your tastebuds every now and then.

Sustainable Living

We are proud of our sustainable practices, We recycle our wash water and cooking oil. We only use bath ammeneties made from natural ingredients and we are always running weekly beach cleaning campaigns with the local community.
